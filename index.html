问题定义 Problem Definition

学生不一定缺少能力，他们常常缺少可迁移的压力调节方法。 Students may not lack ability. They often lack transferable stress regulation methods.

在高考等高压环境中，焦虑、倦怠、注意力下降和考场空白会影响已经掌握知识的学生。稳考 Plan 将压力看作学习与表现问题，而不只是情绪问题。 In high pressure contexts such as Gaokao preparation, anxiety, burnout, reduced attention, and blanking out can affect students who already know the content. Resilience Planner treats stress as a learning and performance issue, not only an emotional issue.

🧠

工作记忆受限Working Memory Strain

压力升高时，学生更难保持注意力和处理复杂信息。When stress rises, learners may struggle to sustain attention and process complex information.

⏱️

限时任务焦虑Timed Task Anxiety

倒计时会放大紧张感，让原本会做的题变得更难。Countdown pressure can intensify anxiety and make familiar tasks feel harder.

🪫

长期备考倦怠Preparation Burnout

持续高强度学习容易造成疲惫、自我怀疑和低效复习。Sustained intensive preparation can produce fatigue, self doubt, and inefficient study routines.

🧭

缺少个人策略Lack of Personal Strategy

学生知道自己紧张，却未必知道哪一种方法适合自己。Students may know they are stressed without knowing which strategy fits their situation.

学习路径Learning Journey

从意识到行动，再到真实情境中的迁移。 From awareness, to action, to real life transfer.

App 按照 10 个微任务组织，每个任务 5 到 8 分钟，服务于课堂后的持续练习和反思。 The app is organized around ten micro tasks, each taking five to eight minutes, supporting continued practice and reflection after class.

学生端功能Student Features

不是多一个打卡工具，而是一个可反馈的韧性学习系统。 Not another check in tool, but a feedback based resilience learning system.

每个功能都服务于一个教学目的：识别模式，练习策略，迁移到真实学习场景。 Each feature serves an instructional purpose: identifying patterns, practicing strategies, and transferring learning into authentic academic situations.

📍

今日状态记录Daily Stress Check In

学生快速记录压力水平、触发因素、身体反应和当前学习状态。Students quickly log stress level, triggers, body signals, and current learning state.

🗺️

个人压力地图Personal Stress Map

系统将记录转化为可视化模式，帮助学生看见什么时候、为什么、如何紧张。The system turns logs into visual patterns so students can see when, why, and how stress appears.

🧰

策略工具箱Strategy Toolkit

提供腹式呼吸、认知重评、注意力重启和考前稳定流程。Includes diaphragmatic breathing, cognitive reframing, focus reset, and pre exam stabilization routines.

📝

韧性计划生成器Resilience Plan Builder

学生把压力模式、策略选择和可测量目标整合成自己的 Academic Resilience Plan。Students integrate stress patterns, strategy choices, and measurable goals into an Academic Resilience Plan.

压力模拟器Pressure Simulator

通过短时间任务、调节策略和二次尝试，学生可以直接体验压力如何影响表现，也能观察策略是否真的帮助自己恢复注意力。Through short timed tasks, regulation strategies, and a second attempt, students can directly experience how stress affects performance and whether a strategy helps them regain focus.
